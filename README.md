StadiumPulse Ops Centre
🏟️ The Mission
StadiumPulse is an AI-powered operations dashboard engineered for the FIFA World Cup 2026. It transforms massive, real-time sensor streams into actionable intelligence to ensure spectator safety and operational efficiency during high-capacity events.
🚀 Why StadiumPulse?
Managing 80,000+ spectators is a high-stakes challenge. Most legacy systems are reactive and prone to human error. StadiumPulse leverages Generative AI to provide predictive insights, allowing operators to identify bottlenecks and divert crowd flow before a surge becomes a safety risk.
✨ Key Features
Real-Time Tactical Map: Dynamic heatmaps visualizing spatial density and spectator movement.
AI Operations Co-Pilot: A Gemini-powered assistant that analyzes telemetry logs to provide automated crowd-routing protocols.
Unified Telemetry Feed: Centralized monitoring of turnstiles, transport shuttles, and concession density.
Incident Response Hub: Automated logging and tracking for operational anomalies.
Enterprise-Grade UI: High-performance, dark-mode glassmorphic design optimized for high-stress operational environments.
🛠️ Technical Stack
Framework: Streamlit (Deployed on Streamlit Cloud)
AI Engine: Google Gemini (Gemini-2.5-flash)
State Management: Optimized session-state synchronization for seamless multi-page interactivity.
Styling: Custom CSS/HTML injection for bespoke dashboard aesthetics.
